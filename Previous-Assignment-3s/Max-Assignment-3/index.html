The Capitol Broadcast Team

Communication is key. Ham radios, audio/video, you name it, we do it.

What is the Capitol Broadcast Team

The Capitol Broadcast Team is a group of Capitol Technology University students interested in the fundamental thing that made this school what it is—communications. Going back to the school's roots when it was known as the Capitol Radio Engineering Institute, the Capitol Broadcast Team aims to not only work with, research, and educate on radio communications but all forms communications.

What to Find Here

This website contains URLs dedicated to all things broadcast. At the highest level, this website contains general information about broadcast technologies, a page for nominations and voting for Capitol's resident movie watching club (The Nightwatchers), a page for students of Capitol Technology University to sell and trade broadcast equiment/technology, and information about Capitol Technology University itself.

We hope you have fun and learn a lot on this website!
