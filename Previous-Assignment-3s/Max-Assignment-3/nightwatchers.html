The Nightwatchers

The Nightwatchers is a club at Capitol Technology University that aims to bring students of all majors together as a community of individuals who enjoy the visual arts. This is accomplished by letting them bond through experiencing enjoyable films together every week in the auditorium to escape from the daily stress of college.

Nominations and Voting

Every week, club members of the Nightwatchers can individually nominate a movie they'd like to see screened. These nominations consist of the movie title, the year (if applicable in cases such as remakes of similar names), and a trailer/description/argument as to why the movie being nominated should be voted for.
